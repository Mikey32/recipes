Ingredients:
 - 1/4 cup butter
 - 1 tablespoon salt
 - 2 sweet potatoes
 - 1/4 cup cocoanut oil

Directions:
  1. Place frying pan on medium heat adding cocoanut oil.
  2. Shred potatoes and add when butter is almost melted.
  3. Add butter on top of potatoes.
  4. Cook until potatoes are at desired crispiness. Takes 8 - 15 minutes.
